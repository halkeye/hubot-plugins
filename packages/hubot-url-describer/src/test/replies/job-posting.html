			About the Role:

Serves as a specialist in the development, implementation and administration of internal facility security.  Develops, implements and documents security processes and procedures and manuals as the company's designated security officer and maintains appropriate levels of controls. In this role you will have oversight of safety and security of our downtown Austin facility and employees.

What are you going to do?

* Proactively develops and implements security processes and procedures for RMN;
* Where safety and continuity procedures don't exist, writes them and socializes with key stakeholders;
* Assists in the implementation and administration of automated security systems;
* Maintains records for periodic secure systems inspections with customer liaisons and other technical professionals;
* Creates business continuity procedures for our business to operate under adverse conditions such as a major storm or power outage;
* Performs periodic and random security inspections and prepares security reports as necessary;
* Patrols all areas of our property, monitor activity with a eye towards safety and loss prevention, addresses and resolves issues as appropriate;
* Manages employee parking, security badges, etc;
* Responds to incidents and notify appropriate individuals internally and call for outside assistance if necessary;
* Escorts any unwelcome persons from the property without interrupting normal business operations;
* Conducts security awareness training sessions with team members;
* Others duties as assigned.

Experiences/Qualifications

* Must be proficient in developing records systems, reports, and secure system processing requirements;
* Working knowledge of security requirements, procedures and incident investigation;
* Must have a high school diploma/GED, associates or bachelor's degree preferred with 5 years security experience;
* A customer service attitude, demonstrated by a positive attitude and a professional personal appearance;
* Ability to stand for long periods of time and walk moderate distances;
* Must be able to work a flexible schedule, including weekends and holidays as requested;
* Ability to speak with others using clear professional language;
* Move, lift, carry, push, pull, and place objects weighing less than or equal to 10 pounds without assistance;
* Proficient in basic computer skills;
* CPR/First-aid certifications preferred.
