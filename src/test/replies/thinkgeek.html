Minecraft Papercraft Sets - Paper crafting

An important part of Minecraft is crafting (it's even partly in the name of the game). And now there's a whole new way to craft your Minecraft world: with papercraft kits. Seven different kits await your folding skills - get them all and you can have the Minecraftiest Minecraft Papercraft world ever. It can take over your desk and have snow and animals and hostiles and Steve and minecarts and stickers! Yup, not only do you get to build people and animals and monsters and blocks, but you also get stickers. Stickers make everything better. We've even heard that Creepers smile when you give them stickers. Bring the craftacular fun of building into the real world with these Minecraft Papercraft sets.
Minecraft Papercraft Sets - Paper crafting

Otamatone Deluxe Touch-Sensitive Electronic Musical Instrument - Performance quality.

We carried the Otamatone a few years ago, and it was good. But then it evolved into the aptly named Otamatone Deluxe, and then it was fantastic! The Otamatone Deluxe is bigger than the old version - so big it comes with a neck/shoulder strap to make playing more comfortable. Three pitch levels and a volume control are there, like the regular version. But the best feature, the one that makes it "deluxe," is the line out jack. Plug it into your computer or route it through effect pedals and out yer amp, and you'll easily see why we're in love with the Otamatone all over again. The first one was a toy; the Otamatone Deluxe is a performance-grade instrument, ready to make your rockin' even more unique.
Otamatone Deluxe Touch-Sensitive Electronic Musical Instrument - Performance quality.

Game of Thrones Direwolf Pup Plush - Aaaarrrrrroooooo!

Smart Mass Kinetic Sand - A sandcastle in a can

Kinetic Sand is super weird. It is 98% real sand and 2% magic. That magic binds the sand together in a unique way. See, you can squish it and mold it like perfect sand castle-building sand. It will hold its shape and stand proud on your desk. But that's only part of the magic! Gently press the sand, and it will collapse - almost flowing like liquid. And because Kinetic Sand really only sticks to itself, cleanup is as simple as squishing bigger bits onto the smaller bits. Keep a tin in your desk and you can relieve stress the natural way: at the beach!
Smart Mass Kinetic Sand - A sandcastle in a can

Reverse and Single Hand Watches - Time is out of joint

Now that we all carry devices around with us that have the time on them, hardly anybody relies exclusively on an analog watch. So why not let your watch do something other than tell time? Let it broadcast your tongue-in-cheek style as well. These two watches, one that runs backwards and the other which tells time with just one hand, will amuse your friends and bemuse your enemies. And woe be to any ignorant meeting attendees who futilely try to use your wrist to gauge when they can make their escape.
Reverse and Single Hand Watches - Time is out of joint

ROCCAT Studios out of Hamburg, Germany specializes in producing high-end gaming peripherals with incredible attention-to-detail. Our gaming buyer calls them "The BMW of gaming gear." We've picked up some of their gaming peripherals, both on the high-end and entry-level, including mice, keyboards, headsets, and more. But remember they're not just for gaming. Any power-user will have an improved experience with these tools.

Six Shooters Ice Shots - Freeze or I'll shoot!

After a hard day of riding the range or shootin' zombies, it's good to kick back at your local saloon and cool your parched throat. Sounds like it's high time to take this Six Shooters Ice Shot mold out for a spin. Just fill this silicone tray (shaped like a revolver's cylinder) with water, put it in the freezer, and in an hour or two you'll be unmolding a half-a-dozen shotglasses designed to keep a shot of your favorite alcohol on ice.
